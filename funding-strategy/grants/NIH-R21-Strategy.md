## Potential Weaknesses & Mitigation

### Weakness 1: Limited Experimental Background
**Mitigation:**
- Strong computational focus (strength of R21)
- Clinical collaborator provides experimental expertise
- Focus on publicly available datasets (reproducible)

### Weakness 2: IIT Remains Controversial
**Mitigation:**
- Agnostic framework (works with any consciousness theory)
- Empirical validation (data-driven)
- Published software demonstrates feasibility

### Weakness 3: No Prior NIH Funding
**Mitigation:**
- R21 designed for new investigators
- Strong preliminary data (software + initial results)
- Clear feasibility demonstration
